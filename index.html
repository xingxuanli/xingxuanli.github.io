About me

I am a research scientist at MiroMind, where I work on advancing large language models (LLMs), large multimodal models (LMMs), and agentic systems. More specifically, my current research focuses on LLM / LMM reasoning and agentic tool use.

Prior to joining MiroMind, I completed my Ph.D. through a joint program between Nanyang Technological University and Alibaba DAMO Academy. I was fortunate to be advised by Assoc. Prof. Shafiq Joty and Dr. Lidong Bing. During my Ph.D., my research centered on enhancing knowledge-augmented reasoning and safety of LLMs. Earlier in my academic journey, I earned a Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) in AI from Computer Science and Design (CSD) at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), fully funded by the Singapore Ministry of Education and SUTD through the SM2 scholarship. I then spent one and half year working as a quantitative analyst in a commodity trading house and completed a Master of Computing degree at National University of Singapore (NUS).

We are actively looking for research interns and full-time researchers. We offer: (1) a supportive and collaborative research environment, (2) sufficient GPU resources, and (3) competitive compensation. If you're interested in working with us, feel free to reach out!
